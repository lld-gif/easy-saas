## Quick Logo
Don't spend money on a logo yet. Use one of these:
1. **Text logo:** Your product name in the heading font, bold, with your primary color
2. **Emoji logo:** Pick a relevant emoji and use it as your favicon + social icon
3. **AI generated:** Use DALL-E or Midjourney for a simple icon when you're ready

## Brand Voice
Keep it **simple, friendly, and direct.** You're building for people, not enterprises. Write like you're explaining to a friend.
