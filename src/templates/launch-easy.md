## Launch Day Plan

### Before Launch
- [ ] Working app deployed to a public URL
- [ ] One-page landing page explaining what it does
- [ ] Screenshot or short demo GIF
- [ ] Create accounts on Product Hunt, Reddit, X

### Launch Day
- [ ] Post on Product Hunt (schedule for 12:01 AM PT)
- [ ] Post on Reddit: r/SideProject, r/Entrepreneur, r/SaaS
- [ ] Tweet/post on X with a demo GIF
- [ ] Ask 5 friends to try it and give feedback

### First Week
- [ ] Respond to every piece of feedback
- [ ] Fix the top 3 bugs people report
- [ ] Post a "lessons learned" thread on X
- [ ] Reach out to 3 niche communities where your users hang out
